Global hypersurfaces of section for geodesic flows on convex hypersurfaces
Co-authored with Sunghae Cho, Archiv der Mathematik, July 2024.

Here is the arXiv version, which will be updated in due course.

Abstract: We construct a global hypersurface of section for the geodesic flow of a convex hypersurface in Euclidean space admitting an isometric involution. This generalizes the Birkhoff annulus to higher dimensions.
