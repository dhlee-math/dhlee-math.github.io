Global hypersurfaces of section for geodesic flows on convex hypersurfaces

written with Sunghae Cho, Archiv der Mathematik, July 2024
Here is Arxiv version, which will be editted in some time.
Abstract : We construct a global hypersurface of section for the geodesic flow of a convex hypersurface in Euclidean space admits an isometric involution. This generalizes the Birkhoff annulus to higher dimensions.
